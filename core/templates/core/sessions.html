Our Sessions

Explore our yoga and yoga therapy sessions to find the option that best supports your wellbeing, movement, relaxation, and personal goals.

Yoga Sessions

Our yoga sessions are suitable for adults, seniors, and teens who want to improve flexibility, strength, posture, breathing, and general wellbeing in a supportive group environment.

These sessions are a good choice if you are looking for regular movement, relaxation, and gentle guidance through structured yoga classes.

Yoga Therapy Sessions

Yoga therapy sessions offer a more personalised approach, using yoga-based techniques to support physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing.

These sessions may be more suitable if you are looking for focused support with stress, recovery, mobility, or your individual wellbeing needs.
